About

Muvïc allows a user select a pdf of sheet music from your collection. Want more sheet music in your collection? Muvïc allows you to upload your music sheets PDF or lets you explore a variety of sheets available for download from the shop. Once you have selected a piece to play, Muvïc will ask for access to your device's camera. Once Muvïc recognizes and maps your face, you will be able to use facial cues to move through the music sheets. You can try it yourself by raise your eyebrows to progress to the next section. Conversely, you can go to the previous part by furrowing your eyebrows.
